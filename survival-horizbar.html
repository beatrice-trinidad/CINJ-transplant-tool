  1. Day of Diagnosis

    If you recieve a transplant at this time:

    Without transplant:

    Your Personal Survival Rates for Transplantation

    How is this calculated?

    Based on your personal medical records, these charts are supposed to give you and your doctor a better idea of your likelihoods for survival. The doctors at Rutgers Cancer Institute of New Jersey have developed a system for calculating risk that is specific to you and your medical information, including relevant disease-related characteristics, genetics, risk factors, and personal characteristics.

    Learn more about the CINJ algorithm


    How do I read this data?

    The teal green area of color is to give you an idea of how you might respond to the transplant out of a 100% chance of survival.
